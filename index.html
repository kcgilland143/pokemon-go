Welcome to Pokemon Stay! Use the arrow keys or your mouse to navigate the map. When you see a wild Pokemon, click on it to catch it! This will take you to battle mode. Click on the pouch (the backpack icon at the top) and choose a Pokemon. In the battle mode, choose whether to attack or try to catch the Pokemon. Hint: it is easiest to catch a wild Pokemon when its HP (health points) score is lower, but be careful not to kill it or your own Pokemon! Good Luck!
